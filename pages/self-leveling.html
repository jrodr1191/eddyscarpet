Why Choose Self-Leveling Flooring?

Self leveling flooring is primarily useful when you have cracked, uneven, or just plain broken concrete floors, although this option can still be utilized effectively on a variety of different flooring, such as VCT, vinyl, wood, and much more. Self-leveling concrete is poured over the desired surface to (as the name suggests) level the floor, and create a smooth and brand new surface made to last.

The installation process is extremely simple, and the product will normally dry within 2-6 hours after application. After the drying is complete, various modifications can be made to make the concrete appear more aesthetic, such as adding rugs, carpets, furniture, and even adding dyes to the floor to really make it pop. At the end of the day, if you need a solid, strong, and flat surface that will take a short amount of time to complete, and still offer great flexibility in how you customize your floor after, then the self-leveling approach may be the right choice for you.
