Repairs Done Fast and Affordable

We don't only focus on installing new floors, and make sure to put the save love and quality into our repairs as the rest of our work. Having luxury vinyl or unique and expressive carpets that extend across a room can be a make or break that helps tie everything else together. With that said, having such flooring for extended amounts of time and across large spaces eventually causes build up that may cause damages to said flooring, ranging from small irreversible scratches to rips and tears in the fabric itself.

Why Repair?

Replacing flooring is expensive, whether it be to install a new carpet, or just have simplistic tile or vinyl replace your current floors. Occasionally, someone might want to keep an old carpet, but because of some wear and tear in certain areas, they might feel the need to give it attention and finally do something about it. Our flooring repair services aim to not only fix problem areas in the floor, but to hopefully give the feel of a completely new floor at a fraction of the price. We specialize in carpet repair, but also work with repairing tile, vinyl, laminate, and more. Call or email us today to find out how we can be of service to you!
